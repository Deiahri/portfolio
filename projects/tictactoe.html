TicTacToe Bot

A Simple AI Bot - Complete

Goal

Design and implement an AI bot that can play TicTacToe, and a front-end for the application to be used.

Implementation

Used Artificial intelligence concepts, such as Mini-max search, Alpha-beta pruning, and a standard Utility-based agent to design the skeleton of the agent.

Initially designed in Python, but converted to JavaScript to allow it to be accessable through a website.
